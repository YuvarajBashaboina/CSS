Nick Vujicic

Nick Vujicic is a world-renowned motivational speaker, evangelist, and author from Australia. He was born in 1982 in Melbourne with tetra-amelia syndrome, a rare disorder characterized by the absence of all four limbs.Despite the immense physical challenges, he has inspired millions of people around the world with his message of hope, perseverance, and faith.

Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking (1942 – 2018) was one of the most brilliant theoretical physicists and cosmologists of our time. Despite living with a severe physical disability, he made groundbreaking contributions to science and inspired the world with his determination.

Helen Keller

Helen Keller (1880 – 1968) was an extraordinary American author, activist, and lecturer who overcame severe disabilities to inspire millions around the world.When she was 7 years old, a teacher named Anne Sullivan came into her life. Anne patiently taught Helen how to understand words by spelling them into her hand. The first word Helen learned was “water”, and from then on she began to learn quickly.

Sudha Chandran

Sudha Chandran is a famous Indian dancer and actress. She was born in 1965 in Kerala. From childhood, she loved dancing and worked very hard to become a Bharatanatyam .She later became a successful actress in movies and TV shows, and is admired for her strong willpower.Sudha Chandran’s story teaches us that even big problems cannot stop us if we have passion, determination, and hard work.
